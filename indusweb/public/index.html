What We Offer

We provide specialized solutions in the area of Industrial automation and manufacturing special purpose machines. We cater to the customized requirements of both domestic and international clients in the domain of oil, food and beverages, sheet metal and powder coating industries.

Automated Material Handling Systems

We are specialized in automated material handling systems by integrating load sensors, vision systems, Robotic systems etc.
With the expertise in Collaborative Robotics, Indusbot can help its customers to achieve work class manufacturing very economically. We ensure the lean Manufacturing process from the design stage to the implementation stage, after rigorous study of customer processes and products.

Pretreatment Transporter & Conveyor Systems

Indusbot provides customized solutions for pretreatment for Powder Coating, ED Coating & Painting. The transporter system can be integrated with a fully automatic chemical doping & water treatment systems.
A Conveyor is provided for handling objects of any size and dimensions. We also integrate with robotic coating systems and provide a fully automatic Coating solution for our customers.

Hoisting & Crane Solutions

Indusbot manufactures highly reliable electric wire rope hoists suitable of lifting 1 ton to 25 tons capacity. EOT, Gantry & JIB cranes are designed and fabricated as per customer requirement.
The Electric wire rope is fully designed and manufactured by Indusbot. A strict process like stress relieving & heat treatment is followed throughout manufacturing. Motors, Bearings & Ropes are sourced from reputed manufacturers and inspection is carried in all stages of manufacturing to ensure life-long trouble-free operation of the Hoist.
The design conforms to IS 3938:1983 rev 2003.

Cargo Lifts

Indusbot has developed a reliable and robust solution for moving cargo vertically in manufacturing facilities and warehouses. These lifts are designed, in capacities and platform sizes, as per the customer requirement to meet the infrastructure at site.
The design and safety factors of the list conform to BIS CED 46/T-18 (Draft) and IS 14665:2005 standards.

Hydrauilic Cargo Lifts & Scissor Lifts

Hydraulic system offers energy efficient and reliable solutions of lifting. They are compact and suitable for heavy loads. Scissor lifts are suitable for large platform sizes and heavy loads like automobiles.
The Hydrauilic Lift manufactured by Indusbot conforms to IS 14671:1999 standard.

Trolley, Pallet Trucks, Pallet Trolley...

We deal in a wide range of Trolleys, Pallet Trolleys and Pallet Trucks which are sourced from reliable suppliers. Customer specific designs are fabricated in our own factory. This makes Indusbot a one-stop source for all your requirements for Material Handling.
